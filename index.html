About

Hardware meets
software

I'm drawn to projects that live at the intersection of electronics and code — where you have to understand both layers to make something work. I've built custom keyboards from bare PCBs, designed energy monitoring hardware from schematic to firmware, and wound a brushless motor by hand.

Outside of engineering I'm into autumn aesthetics, 3D modelling, and making things that are as considered in design as they are in function.

Beyond Engineering
